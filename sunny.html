The Birth (and Death) Of Solar Systems

LStars, like gaseous planets, contains mainly hydrogen and helium. They are actually made up of around 87% hydrogen, and only 10% helium. They also contain 3% of other elements, such as calcium. We can figure out what exactly a star is made of using their spectrum lines.
